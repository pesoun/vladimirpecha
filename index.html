Personas

PERSONAS are documents describing typical target users. Designing my personas I paid attention on simplicity, clarity and most importantly practicality. Therefore, left part of each persona document serves as an immediate overview of each character. Section on the right illustrates detailed information including their bio which will help with designing part.

Wireframes

Wireframing is a process of designing a digital service at the structural level. It is used to lay out functionality and content of websites or apps and they are used early in the development process to establish structure before visual design is added.  Wireframes presented here illustrates what happens when device is moved from left side to the right one. 

Looking at my personas with various technical skills, I knew I need to come up with something intuitive and easy to use. Therefore, I decided not to give users an option of touching the screen. All interaction with the device will happen when the whole device is moved (horizontally and vertically).

Customer Journey Map

Customer Journey Map (CJM) is visual representation of every experience customer have with certain product or company. It helps businesses to get into customer and see their business from customer’s point of view. As with personas also here I tried to represent customer journey map as simple as possible so everyone can understand it. That’s why, in my CJM, I tried to experiment a little when putting all personas to one list.
